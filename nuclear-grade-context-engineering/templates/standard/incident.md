**Purpose:** Run a live incident the stabilize-first way and preserve the truth of what happened: one commander, facts separated from hypotheses, reversible-first actions, a steady comms cadence, and corrective actions driven to closure.

**Activation threshold:** Use when production is down or degraded, data is at risk, security is in question, users are harmed, or an agent action caused harm.

**Minimum useful version:** the commander and roles, the timeline with fact/hypothesis labels, the decisions, the comms cadence, and corrective actions with owners and closure triggers.

- The timeline separated confirmed facts from hypotheses throughout.
- The live phase closed only when stable, with every corrective action owned and given a closure trigger.
