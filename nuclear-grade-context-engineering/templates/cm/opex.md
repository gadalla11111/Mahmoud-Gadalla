**Purpose:** Turn lessons from real operation (OPEX) — user feedback, near misses, incidents, or review surprises — into lasting updates.

**Activation threshold:** Use when operation teaches something that should change the basis, tests, controls, thresholds, docs, or baseline.

**Minimum useful version:** the event, the impact, the evidence, the weak or missing control, the fix, the verification, and what happens to the baseline.

**Overhead trap:** Do not write a postmortem just for show. The lesson must change something or be clearly closed out.

## Exit criteria

- Each finding has an action or a clear closure.
- A closure with no lasting update explains why no basis, test, validator, template, skill, command, monitor, doc, or baseline change is needed.
- Any trigger for a new baseline is recorded.
- Later reviewers can see what changed because of the lesson.
