## 29 Skills by Domain

### Foundation
- **brand-context** — Capture brand DNA: identity, audience, positioning, values, voice
- **brand-strategy** — Client questionnaire → comprehensive strategy report
- **brand-naming** — Generate names with rationale or evaluate existing options
- **brand-manifesto** — Belief-driven manifesto: what brand stands for and fights against

### Identity & Voice
- **brand-identity** — Visual brief: logo, color, typography, imagery, design principles
- **brand-voice** — Verbal identity: tone, vocabulary, writing style, channel adaptations
- **brand-story** — Origin/founder narratives in three lengths (long, short, one-liner)
- **brand-guidelines** — Comprehensive standards doc: logo, color, type, voice, applications

### Market Position
- **brand-positioning** — Competitive landscape map + positioning statement + proof points
- **brand-messaging** — Messaging hierarchy: taglines, value proposition, audience-specific variants
- **target-audience** — Deep personas: psychographics, behavioral patterns, language analysis
- **competitor-branding** — Map rival branding → identify gaps and differentiation opportunities

### Growth & Lifecycle
- **brand-launch** — Public debut: pre-launch assets, launch sequence, momentum plan
- **rebranding** — Strategic transformation: diagnosis, equity audit, rollout planning
- **brand-audit** — Health check: positioning, visuals, messaging, voice, audience alignment
- **brand-measurement** — KPIs, metrics, tracking systems for brand health and equity
- **brand-architecture** — Portfolio structure: relationships between brands and product lines
- **personal-brand** — Founder positioning, POV, platform, 90-day activation plan

### Channel & Revenue
- **d2c-marketing** — Unit economics, channels, flows, launch checklist
- **b2b-brand-marketing** — Buying committee messaging, thought leadership, ABM
- **brand-partnerships** — Co-branding: identification, outreach, protection
- **brand-packaging** — Packaging brief: structure, visuals, hierarchy, materials, unboxing
- **ugc-strategy** — UGC campaigns, creator briefs, FTC compliance
- **influencer-marketing** — Tier selection, vetting, contracts, FTC compliance
- **email-marketing** — List building, segmentation, campaigns, benchmarks
- **whatsapp-marketing** — Setup, campaigns, flows, conversational commerce
- **meta-ads** — Facebook/Instagram: audience targeting, creative, ROAS optimization
- **google-ads** — Search, Shopping, Performance Max: keywords + bidding strategy
- **aso** — App Store Optimization: keywords, screenshots, A/B testing
