## Schwartz awareness workflow

```
1. avatar-extraction → who is the buyer?
2. schwartz-awareness-mapper → where are they in the funnel?
3. mechanism-builder → what makes us uniquely credible?
4. ad-angle-multiplier → generate 10 angles
5. headline-matrix → write 20 headlines
6. scroll-stopping-creative → hook for best angle
7. objection-crusher → handle top 3 objections in body
8. conversion-path-builder → landing page + CTA
9. performance-diagnosis → iterate on underperformers
```
